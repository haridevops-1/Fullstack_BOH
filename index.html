Why I Built This

Hello! I am Hariharan Ravi. During my time at the academy, I noticed a deep gap between the surplus food wasted daily and the communities going hungry. I built BridgeOfHope to bridge this very gap—leveraging technology to make donating surplus food straightforward, transparent, and instantly impactful. My hope is to create a community where no meal is wasted and no plate is empty.
